Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01 · Jordan Snyder

This Privacy Policy describes how Jordan Snyder ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when you interact with our SMS scheduling assistant.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile phone number — collected when you text our number or when Jordan Snyder adds you as a client.
  • Message content — the text of messages you send to and receive from our scheduling assistant.
  • Scheduling data — your appointment history, session preferences, package balance, and related notes.
  • Carrier metadata — your wireless carrier may transmit standard data such as message timestamps and delivery receipts.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above only to:

  • Schedule, confirm, reschedule, and cancel personal-training sessions;
  • Send appointment reminders and operational notifications;
  • Respond to your questions about scheduling and availability;
  • Maintain accurate client records.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or message content with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with:

  • Twilio Inc., our SMS messaging provider, solely to deliver messages between you and us. See Twilio's Privacy Notice.
  • Service providers that host or back up our scheduling database under confidentiality obligations.
  • Authorities when required by law (e.g. valid subpoena).

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain message logs and scheduling records for as long as you remain an active client and for a reasonable period afterward to support business records and dispute resolution. You may request deletion at any time by emailing jordan.snyder@gmail.com.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages.
  • Get help by replying HELP or by emailing jordan.snyder@gmail.com.
  • Request access or deletion of your data by emailing the address above.

7. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The current version is always posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

Jordan Snyder · jordan.snyder@gmail.com
